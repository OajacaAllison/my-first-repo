About Me

Good morning, afternoon or night. I'm Allison—a Guatemalan student whose story plays like a carefully curated album collection. Every track captures a different part of me: art, music, tech, and design. I’ve always been drawn to creativity, whether through architecture sketches, media projects, or playing piano. Music is my everyday background—it helps me focus, process, and express myself. Below, you'll find a few of my favorite “albums”—snapshots of who I am, what I’ve built, and where I’m going. From robotics to graduating high school and diving deep into computer science, each one plays a part in shaping my future.

I was apart of Pike High School’s FIRST® robotics team, the RoboDevils 1018. As Business and Media Lead, I managed design, marketing, and photography—blending creativity with leadership to help our team stand out.

After graduating from Pike, I’m heading to the University of Cincinnati for architecture. Right now, I’m part of the Blatina Varsity Architecture Program, working on real projects with architects from RATIO.

This summer, I’m in the Nextech Catapult program, learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s a hands-on tech experience that’s helped me grow my coding skills and connect with mentors in the field.
