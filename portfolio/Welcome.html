About Me

Good morning, afternoon or night. My name is Allison! A Guatemalan student whose life plays out like a collection of records. Each one represents a layer of who I am: an artist, a pianist, a designer, and a future architect. Music is my constant—it inspires my artwork, helps me focus when I’m building something new, and lets me connect with others in meaningful ways. My favorite moments usually involve sketchbooks, good lighting, and a song playing in the background. My love for history, architecture, and storytelling blends into everything I do, and I’m excited to turn all that into a career that’s as creative as it is impactful.

In high school, I participated in my high schools's first robotics club. We were the Pike Robodevils 1018. I was part of it for 3 years and was promoted to business and media lead during my 2nd year. I've managed and created projects which included graphic design, digital marketing, photography, etc.

I recently graduated and plan to attend University of Cincinnati as an architecture major. I'm currently doing an architecture program called Blatina Varsity Architecture, which allows me to get experience with real architects from Ratio firm.

I am also attending another summer program called Nextech Catapults, a comp sci program that has taught me HTML, CSS and Javascript.
