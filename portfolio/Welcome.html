About Me

I’m Allison—a Guatemalan student whose life plays out like a collection of records. Each one represents a layer of who I am: an artist, a pianist, a designer, and a future architect. Music is my constant—it inspires my artwork, helps me focus when I’m building something new, and lets me connect with others in meaningful ways. My favorite moments usually involve sketchbooks, good lighting, and a vinyl playing in the background. My love for history, architecture, and storytelling blends into everything I do, and I’m excited to turn all that into a career that’s as creative as it is impactful.
